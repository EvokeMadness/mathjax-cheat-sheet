Tex delimiters:   $...$   or   $$...$$   or   \(...\)   or   \[...\]  
NOTE: Some delimiters and text placement options may not be available depending on the Tex environment.

- **Operators**
	- $\times$
	- $\cdot$
	- $\div$
	- $\frac {x}{y}$

- **Formatting**
	- $\{\ \}$
	- $a^b$
	- $a_b$

- **More Operators**
	- $\sqrt x$ or $\sqrt[n] x$
	- $\int_a^bc$
	- $\sum_a^bc$ or $\sum\limits_a^bc$
	- $\prod_a^bc$ or $\prod\limits_a^bc$

- **Symbols**
	- $\pm$
	- $\approx$
 	- $\leftarrow$
	- $\rightarrow$
	- $\infty$
	- $\pi$
	- $\tau$

- **Inequalities**
	- $\ge$ or $\geq$
	- $\le$ or $\leq$
	- $\ne$ or $\neq$
